Legend

Roles

  1. Sheriff Kill all Outlaws and the Renegade.
  2. Deputy Protect the Sheriff.
  3. Outlaw Kill the Sheriff.
  4. Renegade Be the last one alive.

Health

  1. Heath How many cards you can have at the end of your turn.
  2. Lost Health Lost heath you can regain.

Figures

  1. Bart Cassidy Lose health, draw a card.
  2. Black Jack Show second draw, if red suit, draw another.
  3. Calamity Janet Bangs can be played as Misses, and Misses as Bangs.
  4. El Gringo Draws card from player damaged him.
  5. Jesse Jones Can draw first card from other player.
  6. Joudonais Has a barrel at all times.
  7. Kit Carlson Draw 3, keep 2, put 1 back.
  8. Lucky Duke Flip 2 cards on a draw and choose 1.
  9. Paul Regret Has a Mustang at all times.
  10. Pedro Ramierz Can draw from discard.
  11. Rose Doolan Has an Scope at all times.
  12. Sid Ketchum Can discard 2 cards for 1 life.
  13. Slab The Killer Others need 2 misses to counter his Bang!.
  14. Suzy Lafayette Draw a card when hand is empty.
  15. Vulture Sam Takes all cards from dead players.
  16. Willy the Kid No limit on Bangs per turn.

Suits

  1. Hearts
  2. Diamonds
  3. Clubs
  4. Spades

Guns

  1. Colt .45 Gun range of 1. Default gun.
  2. Volcanic Gun range of 1. Play any number of bangs.
  3. Schofield Gun range of 2.
  4. Remington Gun range of 3.
  5. Winchester Gun range of 4.
  6. Rev. Carbine Gun range of 5.

Items

  1. Scope Other players look 1 space closer.
  2. Barrel Draw on being Banged. If heart counts as Missed.
  3. Dynamite Draw before Drawing cards. If 2-9 of spades. Take 3 damage, else pass to next player.
  4. Mustang You look 1 space further from other players.
  5. Jail Draw before Drawing cards. Miss a turn if not Heart.

Play Cards

  1. Bang Damage a player within range. Can play only 1 per turn.
  2. Beer Regain a health. Does not work if only 2 players left.
  3. Cat Balou Other player discards a card from in play of your choosing or random from the hand.
  4. Duel You and any other player discard Bangs, until someone runs out and takes a damage.
  5. Gatling Bang everyone else does not count towards Bang!s per turn.
  6. General Store Everyone gets a card.
  7. Indians Everyone else plays a Bang! or takes a damage.
  8. Missed Counter to Bangs.
  9. Panic Take a card from a player 1 space away.
  10. Saloon Everyone gets a beer.
  11. Stagecoach Draw 2 cards.
  12. Wells Fargo Draw 3 cards.

Others

  1. Discarding or Draw Pile
  2. Card in hand or A players hand
  3. Draw from Discard
  4. Finish discarding 2 for life/finish playing 2 misses
  5. Start Game
  6. Leave Game
  7. Do not respond/finished play phase
  8. Gun
  9. Player
  10. Join Game
